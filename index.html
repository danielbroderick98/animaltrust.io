Welcome to Animal Trust
adoption centre.

Your Perfect Companion is Waiting At Animal Trust,
we believe that every animal deserves a loving home. Our mission is to connect
wonderful pets with compassionate people, creating lifelong bonds that enrich
lives. Whether you're looking for a playful puppy, a cuddly kitten, or a gentle senior
pet, we have a variety of animals ready to find their forever families.



You can donate to us today

Your generous donations make a world of difference, helping us provide life-changing care and forever homes for our furry friends.
Every contribution supports our mission to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need.
We truly appreciate your support—our four-legged companions are grateful too! Together, we can create a brighter future for these deserving animals.
Thank you for being a part of our community!
